We would like to invite men who buy sex in England and Wales to participate in this study. We are interested in their understanding of prostitution legislation, the impact of online and offline experiences on their perception of law enforcement risk, and their concerns over trafficking and exploitation. This will involve conducting interviews with them.

<!--more-->

***

Traditionally, male clients have been absent from sex work policy, and this study will be an opportunity to hear their opinion about policies that directly affect them. The aim of this research is to explore how men who buy sex in England and Wales perceive the main risks associated with it. We are specifically interested in their understanding of prostitution legislation, the impact of online and offline experiences on their perception of law enforcement risk, and their concerns over trafficking and exploitation. This will involve conducting interviews with them.

If you decide to take part, you will be given [this information sheet] to keep and will be asked to sign a consent form to confirm your agreement to participate. You will be able to choose whether you prefer to do the interview face-to-face, via phone, via MS Teams video or audio call, or via instant messenger platforms or applications that support end-to-end encryption such as WhatsApp, Viber and Wickr. You will also be asked whether you would rather talk to a female or a male interviewer. If you would like more information, do not hesitate to [contact us](https://understanding-risk.netlify.app/contact/).
